Trail of a Hundred Giants is located within the Long Meadow Giant Sequoia Grove

The Grove contains 125 giant sequoias over 10 feet in diameter and 143 sequoias under 10 feet in diameter.

About 1.3 miles of paved trail offers several loop options with interpretive signs.

You will view a unique cedar and sequoia tree growing together, and a circle of five sequoias growing together.

It is best to visit in the summer when the road is open, or in the winter by snowmobile.
